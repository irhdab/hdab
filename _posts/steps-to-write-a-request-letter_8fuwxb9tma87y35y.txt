Steps to Write a Request Letter

1. Greeting and Purpose
   - Address the recipient respectfully (e.g., “Dear [Title] [Last Name],”).
   - State your purpose clearly in the opening sentence.

2. Explain the Request
   - Briefly describe what you’re asking for and any key details to clarify your request.

3. Justify the Request
   - Share a relevant reason or benefit for why the request is important or mutually beneficial.

4. Offer Support for Follow-up
   - Let the recipient know you’re available for further discussion or to provide additional information.

5. Closing and Gratitude
   - Thank the recipient in advance, and end with a polite closing (e.g., “Sincerely”) followed by your name.
